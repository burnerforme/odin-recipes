Pizza recipe

Description

This is the recipe for the best pizza.

Ingredients:
  • 1 pound whole-wheat pizza dough
  • 3/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 pound frozen mushrooms, thawed and sliced
  • 1 pound frozen spinach, thawed and sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated feta cheese
Instructions:
  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper.
